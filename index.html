About me

I received the B.S. degree from the Wuhan University, in 2019, and the M.S. degree from Wuhan University, in 2021. I am currently a PhD student with the Institute for artificial Intelligence and machine Perception, Wuhan University, China.
My main research interests include Learned Image/Video Compression. Also, I do some research about Video Processing, such as Video Super-Resolution.
