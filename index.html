About me

I received the B.S. degree from the Wuhan University, in 2019, and the M.S. degree from Wuhan University, in 2021. I am currently a PhD student with the Institute for artificial Intelligence and machine Perception, Wuhan University, China. My main research interests include Machine Learning, Deep Learning and Recommender Systems. Also, I do some research about Computer Vision, such as knee osteoarthritis prediction, and Time Series Prediction, such as remaining useful life prediction of Lithium-ion batteries. I focus on using AI to solve practical problems.
